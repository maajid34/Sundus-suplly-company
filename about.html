About Us

Background Of The Company

Sundus Supply Company, established in 2009, has become a leading trading enterprise in the Gedo region of South-Central Somalia, operating from its headquarters in Belethawa District. Over the past years Gedo region often characterized by economic challenges and instability, Sundus Supply has positioned itself as a critical provider of essential goods and services they required by the community living in Gedo region. The company prides itself on its commitment to quality and exceptional customer service, which has allowed it to cultivate strong relationships with a diverse array of stakeholders, including government entities, local NGOs, and international humanitarian organizations. These partnerships are crucial, as they enable Sundus to understand and respond effectively to the unique needs of the community it serves. The company’s operations not only focus on immediate supply needs but also aim to foster long-term economic resilience in the region. By engaging with local communities and supporting various initiatives, Sundus Supply Company is dedicated to creating a positive impact that transcends mere commerce, contributing to the overall development and stability of the Gedo region.
